OUR STRATEGIES AND SERVICES

Online Presence Management

ChapterX works with countries to craft and strengthen their online presence. We offer modern, responsive, and mobile-optimized redesigns of websites, as well as analytics and strategies to help countries understand what people are saying about them.

Digital Content Strategy

ChapterX provides profesional digital content production and social media campaign strategies. We work with countries to create videos, articles, graphics, and more to showcase the best of what they have to offer.

Influencer Marketing

ChapterX helps countries find brand ambassadors. We generate the right kind of buzz and get the right kinds of partnerships to get people talking.

Activist Marketing

ChapterX deals with post-crises scenarios by algining campaigns with the goodwill of people. Through activist channels such as crowdfunding and petition sharing, we get people to care about helping countries make a difference.
